---
name: Stress notation convention
description: Use tau for stress unless it is the Cauchy stress tensor, which uses sigma
type: feedback
---

Use $\tau$ for stress in all contexts unless the stress is specifically the Cauchy stress tensor, in which case use $\sigma$.

**Why:** User's established notation convention for ice dynamics and continuum mechanics writing.

**How to apply:** When writing equations involving deviatoric stress, basal drag, lateral shear stress, extensional stress, etc., default to $\tau$. Reserve $\sigma$ exclusively for the full Cauchy stress tensor $\sigma_{ij}$.
